Peace TV Bangla is an Islamic television channel that broadcasts religious and educational content in the Bengali language. As part of the Peace TV network, the channel is dedicated to spreading the teachings of Islam, promoting peace, and fostering understanding among viewers. Peace TV Bangla offers a variety of programs, including Quranic recitations, lectures by renowned Islamic scholars, talk shows, and educational series aimed at deepening the understanding of Islamic principles. The channel also addresses social issues from an Islamic perspective and provides guidance on living a life in accordance with Islamic values. Peace TV Bangla is watched by millions of Bengali-speaking Muslims around the world and is known for its commitment to promoting positive values and peaceful coexistence.

Desh TV is a popular Bengali-language television channel in Bangladesh, recognized for its diverse and engaging programming. Launched in 2009, Desh TV offers a wide range of content, including news, entertainment, dramas, talk shows, music programs, and cultural events. The channel is committed to providing reliable news coverage and current affairs programs that keep viewers informed about national and international events. Desh TV also features a variety of entertainment shows, including popular serials, reality shows, and special broadcasts that appeal to a broad audience. With a focus on promoting Bengali culture and values, Desh TV has established itself as a prominent media outlet in Bangladesh, catering to viewers of all ages.

Ekushey TV (ETV) is a leading Bengali-language television channel in Bangladesh, renowned for its wide array of programming that caters to a diverse audience. Launched in 2000, ETV was the first private terrestrial channel in the country and has since become a household name. The channel offers a mix of news, current affairs, entertainment, dramas, talk shows, and educational programs. Known for its credible and timely news coverage, Ekushey TV plays a significant role in shaping public opinion in Bangladesh. The channel also broadcasts cultural programs, documentaries, and special events that highlight the rich cultural heritage of the country. ETV's content is designed to appeal to viewers of all ages, making it one of the most popular channels in Bangladesh.
